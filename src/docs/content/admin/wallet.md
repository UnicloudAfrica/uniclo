:::step{number=1 icon=Wallet title="Wallet Management" navigation="Sidebar > Billing & Pricing > Wallet"}
The wallet page shows all prepaid credit balances across your platform. Users can load credits into their wallet (like topping up a prepaid phone) and spend them on services. You can view balances, top-up history, and switch billing modes between prepaid and postpaid.
:::

:::screenshot{caption="Your Wallet management page"}
:::

:::step{number=2 icon=ArrowRightLeft title="Settlements" navigation="Sidebar > Billing & Pricing > Settlements"}
Settlements reconcile what tenants owe versus what they have earned. At the end of each billing cycle, the platform calculates the balance and settles accounts. Think of it as balancing the books.
:::

:::step{number=3 icon=Receipt title="Payouts" navigation="Sidebar > Billing & Pricing > Payouts"}
Payouts are money going out to your tenants. When tenants earn revenue from their clients, you pay them their share. This page tracks all outgoing payments, amounts, and statuses.
:::

:::step{number=4 icon=FlaskConical title="POC Trials" navigation="Sidebar > Billing & Pricing > Wallet > POC"}
Proof of Concept (POC) trials let prospective customers try the platform with free credits. You can create trial wallets with a set credit amount and expiration date. When the credits run out or the trial expires, the user is prompted to convert to a paid account.
:::

:::callout{type=info}
POC trials are a great way to onboard new customers. Set a reasonable credit limit and trial duration so prospects can meaningfully evaluate the platform without unlimited spend.
:::
