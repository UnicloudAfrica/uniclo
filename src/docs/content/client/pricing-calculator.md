:::step{number=1 icon=Calculator title="Estimate Your Costs" navigation="Sidebar > Pricing Calculator"}
The calculator lets you pick the resources you need -- server size, storage amount, networking options -- and instantly shows you the estimated monthly cost. Mix and match until you find the right balance of power and price.
:::

:::screenshot{caption="Pricing Calculator page"}
:::

:::step{number=2 icon=SlidersHorizontal title="Adjust and Compare" navigation="Sidebar > Pricing Calculator"}
Try different configurations side by side. Need a bigger server? Slide the CPU and memory up and watch the price change in real time. This helps you find the sweet spot -- enough power for your needs without paying for resources you will not use.
:::

:::callout{type=tip}
Not sure what you need? Start with the smallest option and scale up later. It is always cheaper to grow into what you need than to pay for too much from the start.
:::

:::step{number=3 icon=FileText title="Save and Share Estimates" navigation="Sidebar > Pricing Calculator"}
Once you are happy with a configuration, you can save the estimate or share it with your team. This is helpful when you need approval before spending -- just send the estimate to your manager or finance team.
:::
