:::step{number=1 icon=Settings title="Account Settings" navigation="Sidebar > Account (bottom of sidebar)"}
Your personal admin account settings. Update your profile information, change your password, and manage your two-factor authentication. This is about your own admin account specifically.
:::

:::screenshot{caption="Your Account settings page"}
:::

:::step{number=2 icon=Shield title="Two-Factor Authentication" navigation="Account Settings > Security section"}
Two-factor authentication (2FA) adds an extra layer of security to your admin account. After entering your password, you will also need a code from your authenticator app. It is like needing both a key and a PIN to open a safe.
:::

:::callout{type=warning}
Admin accounts have access to everything on the platform. Always keep 2FA enabled to protect your account from unauthorized access.
:::

:::step{number=3 icon=Palette title="Platform Branding" navigation="Platform branding settings"}
Customize how the platform looks — upload your logo, set your brand colors, and configure your favicon. This is the default branding that tenants see before they set up their own.
:::

:::screenshot{caption="Your Platform branding settings"}
:::

:::step{number=4 icon=UserCog title="Admin Users List" navigation="Sidebar > Customer Management > Admin Users"}
View and manage all administrator accounts on the platform. See each admin's name, email, role, and last login date.
:::

:::step{number=5 icon=Plus title="Create Admin User" navigation="Admin Users > Create"}
Add a new administrator to the platform. Fill in their name, email, and assign their permissions. They will receive an email invitation to set up their account.
:::

:::callout{type=tip}
Follow the principle of least privilege — only give admin users the permissions they actually need. You can always add more later.
:::
