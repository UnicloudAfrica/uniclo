:::step{number=1 icon=Users title="View All Leads" navigation="Sidebar > Customer Management > Leads"}
The leads page shows everyone who has expressed interest in your platform. Each lead has a name, contact info, status, and which stage of the pipeline they are in. You can filter and sort to focus on the most promising opportunities.
:::

:::screenshot{caption="Your Leads list with pipeline stages"}
:::

:::step{number=2 icon=Plus title="Create a New Lead" navigation="Sidebar > Customer Management > Leads > Create Lead"}
Click the create button to add a new lead manually. Fill in the contact name, company, email, and any notes about the opportunity. This is useful when you meet a potential customer at an event or receive a referral.
:::

:::step{number=3 icon=ArrowRight title="Move Leads Through Stages" navigation="Sidebar > Customer Management > Leads"}
Leads move through pipeline stages — from initial contact to qualification to closing. Update a lead's stage as conversations progress. This helps your team see where every deal stands at a glance.
:::

:::callout{type=info}
Leads that convert into paying customers can be promoted to tenants directly from this page, saving you from re-entering their information.
:::
