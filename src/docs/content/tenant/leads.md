:::step{number=1 icon=Target title="View Your Leads" navigation="Sidebar > Customer Management > Leads"}
The leads page shows all of your potential customers in one place. Each lead displays a name, contact info, current pipeline stage, and when they were last updated. This is your sales overview.
:::

:::screenshot{caption="Your Leads list page"}
:::

:::step{number=2 icon=Plus title="Create a New Lead" navigation="Sidebar > Customer Management > Leads > New"}
Click "New" to add a lead manually. Fill in the company name, contact person, email, and any notes about what they are looking for. You can also set the initial pipeline stage.
:::

:::step{number=3 icon=ArrowRight title="Move Leads Through the Pipeline"}
Each lead goes through stages as you work with them:

- **New** -- You just added them
- **Qualified** -- They are a good fit for your services
- **Proposal** -- You sent them an offer
- **Negotiation** -- You are working out the details
- **Won** -- They became a paying client
- **Lost** -- They decided not to sign up

Drag leads between stages or update the stage from the lead details page.
:::

:::screenshot{caption="Your Lead pipeline stages"}
:::

:::callout{type=tip}
When a lead reaches the "Won" stage, you can convert them into a full client with one click. All their contact information carries over automatically.
:::

:::step{number=4 icon=Filter title="Filter and Sort Leads"}
Use the filters at the top of the page to narrow your list by stage, date, or assigned team member. This helps you focus on the leads that need attention right now.
:::
