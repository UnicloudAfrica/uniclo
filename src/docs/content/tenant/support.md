:::step{number=1 icon=LifeBuoy title="View Your Tickets" navigation="Sidebar > Support"}
See all your support tickets in one place. Each ticket shows its subject, status, and when it was last updated. Open tickets are the ones still being worked on.
:::

:::screenshot{caption="Your Support tickets list"}
:::

:::step{number=2 icon=Plus title="Create a New Ticket" navigation="Support > Create Ticket"}
Click "Create Ticket" to ask for help. Describe your issue clearly -- the more detail you provide, the faster the support team can help you.
:::

:::step{number=3 icon=MessageSquare title="Reply to a Ticket" navigation="Click any ticket"}
Click on a ticket to see the full conversation. Type your reply and any additional information the support team has requested.
:::

:::step{number=4 icon=Clock title="Track Progress" navigation="Check ticket status"}
Tickets move through statuses: Open, In Progress, and Resolved. You will receive notifications when there are updates.
:::

:::callout{type=tip}
Include screenshots and specific error messages in your tickets. The more information you provide upfront, the faster the resolution.
:::
