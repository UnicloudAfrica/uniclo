:::step{number=1 icon=Package title="Products" navigation="Sidebar > Billing & Pricing > Products"}
Products are the individual things you sell — like CPU hours, gigabytes of storage, or bandwidth. Think of them like items on a restaurant menu. Each product has a name, a description, and belongs to a category. Click "Add Product" to create a new one.
:::

:::screenshot{caption="Your Products list page"}
:::

:::step{number=2 icon=Layers title="Product Families" navigation="Sidebar > Billing & Pricing > Product Families"}
Product families are categories that group related products together. For example, "Compute" might include all CPU and RAM products, while "Storage" includes disk and object storage products. It is like organizing your menu into sections: appetizers, main courses, desserts.
:::

:::step{number=3 icon=DollarSign title="Pricing Tiers" navigation="Sidebar > Billing & Pricing > Pricing"}
This is where you set the actual prices. You can create different pricing tiers for different regions or customer types. Click "Add Pricing" to set a price for a product in a specific region and currency.
:::

:::screenshot{caption="Your Pricing management page"}
:::

:::callout{type=tip}
Tenants can override your prices with their own pricing for their clients. Your prices here are the baseline — the starting point for everyone.
:::

:::step{number=4 icon=Calculator title="Pricing Calculator" navigation="Sidebar > Billing & Pricing > Pricing Calculator"}
The pricing calculator lets you quickly figure out how much a specific setup would cost. Pick the instance type, storage, and bandwidth — it does the math for you. Great for quoting potential customers.
:::

:::step{number=5 icon=Archive title="Inventory" navigation="Sidebar > Billing & Pricing > Inventory"}
Inventory shows what resources are available across your platform. Think of it like checking what is in stock in your warehouse.
:::

:::step{number=6 icon=CreditCard title="Subscription Plans" navigation="Sidebar > Billing & Pricing > Subscription Plans"}
Subscription plans are recurring billing arrangements — like monthly or yearly memberships. Create plans with different features and pricing levels.
:::

:::step{number=7 icon=MapPin title="Colocation Pricing" navigation="Sidebar > Billing & Pricing > Colocation"}
If you offer physical colocation services (renting rack space), manage those prices here.
:::
