---
title: Price Settings
subtitle: Price settings let you control what your clients pay for cloud services. You buy resources at a base cost and sell them at your own price -- the difference is your profit margin. Think of it as setting the price tags in your own store.
prev: { label: Revenue Dashboard, href: /dashboard/docs/revenue }

---

:::step{number=1 icon=DollarSign title="View Current Pricing" navigation="Sidebar > Billing & Revenue > Price Settings"}
The price settings page shows all services and their current prices. You can see the base cost, your selling price, and the profit margin for each item. Everything is laid out in a clear table.
:::

:::screenshot{caption="Your Price Settings overview table"}
:::

:::step{number=2 icon=Edit title="Set Custom Pricing Overrides"}
Click on any service to change its price. Enter your new selling price and the margin updates automatically. You can set overrides for individual services like compute hours, storage per GB, or database instances.
:::

:::step{number=3 icon=Percent title="Understand Your Margins"}
The margin column shows the percentage difference between your cost and your selling price. A healthy margin ensures your business stays profitable. If the margin looks too thin, consider adjusting your prices.
:::

:::screenshot{caption="Your Pricing override editor with margin preview"}
:::

:::callout{type=tip}
Your pricing overrides apply to all clients by default. If you need to offer a special deal to one client, use the discounts feature in the billing section instead of changing your base prices.
:::
