:::step{number=1 icon=BarChart3 title="Open Analytics" navigation="Sidebar > Analytics"}
The analytics page gives you a bird's-eye view of your entire platform. You can see how many instances are running, how much storage is being used, and how active your tenants and clients are. It is like checking the vital signs of your platform.
:::

:::screenshot{caption="Your Analytics overview dashboard"}
:::

:::step{number=2 icon=TrendingUp title="Usage Metrics" navigation="Sidebar > Analytics"}
Usage metrics show you trends over time — are more instances being created this month? Is storage usage growing? These charts help you spot patterns and plan for the future.
:::

:::step{number=3 icon=Activity title="Performance Dashboards" navigation="Sidebar > Analytics"}
Performance dashboards show how well your infrastructure is running. Monitor response times, uptime, and resource utilization across all regions. If something is slowing down, you will see it here first.
:::

:::callout{type=tip}
Check analytics regularly to spot usage trends early. This helps you plan capacity and identify tenants who may need additional resources or support.
:::

:::
