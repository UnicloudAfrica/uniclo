:::step{number=1 icon=Package title="Browse Available Products" navigation="Sidebar > Products"}
The products page lists every service category you can offer -- compute instances, storage volumes, databases, networking components, and more. Each product shows a description and its current availability.
:::

:::screenshot{caption="Your Products catalog page"}
:::

:::step{number=2 icon=Search title="Filter by Category"}
Use the category filters to narrow the list. For example, view only compute products, only storage products, or only networking products. This makes it easy to find exactly what you are looking for.
:::

:::step{number=3 icon=Eye title="View Product Details"}
Click on any product to see its full details -- specifications, pricing tiers, and which regions it is available in. This information helps you decide what to recommend to your clients.
:::

:::screenshot{caption="Your Product details view"}
:::

:::callout{type=info}
The products available to you depend on which regions you have access to. If you do not see a product you expect, check that you have the right regions enabled in your account.
:::
