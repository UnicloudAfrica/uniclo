:::step{number=1 icon=ShieldCheck title="Serverless DR" navigation="Sidebar > Disaster Recovery > Serverless DR"}
Disaster Recovery keeps a backup copy of your important servers in a safe place. If the original goes down, the backup can take over -- like having a spare tire ready to go.
:::

:::step{number=2 icon=Zap title="Protection" navigation="Sidebar > Disaster Recovery > Protection"}
Set up automated backup policies for your servers. Choose how often backups happen and how long they are kept. Sleep soundly knowing your data is safe.
:::

:::screenshot{caption="Your Protection policies page"}
:::

:::step{number=3 icon=ArrowLeftRight title="Migrations" navigation="Sidebar > Disaster Recovery > Migrations"}
The migration tool helps you move servers and data from one place to another. Whether you are moving between regions or from another provider, the wizard guides you step by step.
:::

:::screenshot{caption="Your Migrations list"}
:::

:::step{number=4 icon=Scaling title="Auto Scaling" navigation="Sidebar > Networking > Auto Scaling"}
Auto scaling automatically adds servers when demand increases and removes them when it drops. Like a restaurant that magically adds tables during the dinner rush.
:::

:::step{number=5 icon=Bot title="Infrastructure Agent" navigation="Sidebar > Infrastructure Agent"}
The agent runs inside your servers to provide monitoring, health checks, and management capabilities.
:::

:::callout{type=info}
Advanced services may have additional costs. Check the pricing calculator to understand the costs before enabling them.
:::
