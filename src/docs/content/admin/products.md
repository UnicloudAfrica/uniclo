:::step{number=1 icon=Package title="View All Products" navigation="Sidebar > Billing & Pricing > Products"}
The products page lists every service available on your platform — compute instances, storage, databases, networking, and more. Each product has a name, description, pricing, and which product family it belongs to.
:::

:::screenshot{caption="Your Products catalog"}
:::

:::step{number=2 icon=FolderTree title="Product Families" navigation="Sidebar > Billing & Pricing > Products > Families"}
Product families group related products together. For example, all compute-related products (small, medium, large instances) belong to one family. Families make it easier for tenants and clients to browse and find what they need.
:::

:::step{number=3 icon=Plus title="Create or Edit Products" navigation="Sidebar > Billing & Pricing > Products > Create"}
Click create to add a new product to your catalog. Define the product name, description, assign it to a family, and set the base pricing. You can also edit existing products to update descriptions or adjust configurations.
:::

:::callout{type=tip}
Keep your product catalog clean and well-organized. Clear product names and descriptions help tenants and clients choose the right services without confusion.
:::
