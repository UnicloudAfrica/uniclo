:::step{number=1 icon=LifeBuoy title="View All Tickets" navigation="Sidebar > Support Tickets"}
The tickets page shows all support requests across your platform. Each ticket has a subject, a status (open, in progress, resolved), who submitted it, and when. You can filter and search to find specific tickets quickly.
:::

:::screenshot{caption="Your Support tickets list"}
:::

:::step{number=2 icon=MessageSquare title="Respond to a Ticket" navigation="Click any ticket to open it"}
Click on a ticket to see the full conversation. You can read the customer's message, view any attachments, and type your response. It works like a simple messaging thread.
:::

:::screenshot{caption="Your Ticket conversation view"}
:::

:::step{number=3 icon=Clock title="Track Status" navigation="Use the status dropdown on the ticket"}
Each ticket moves through statuses: Open (new and waiting), In Progress (being worked on), and Resolved (problem solved). Update the status as you work on the issue so the customer knows what is happening.
:::

:::step{number=4 icon=CheckCircle2 title="Close a Ticket" navigation="Set status to Resolved"}
When the issue is resolved, mark the ticket as resolved. The customer will be notified that their issue has been addressed.
:::

:::callout{type=tip}
Quick responses build trust. Even if you cannot solve the issue immediately, a fast acknowledgment lets the customer know their message was received.
:::
