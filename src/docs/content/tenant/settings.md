:::step{number=1 icon=Settings title="Account Settings" navigation="Sidebar > Account (bottom)"}
Update your profile information -- name, email, phone number, and company details. This is your personal account configuration.
:::

:::screenshot{caption="Your Account settings page"}
:::

:::step{number=2 icon=Shield title="Security & 2FA" navigation="Account Settings > Security"}
Change your password and set up two-factor authentication (2FA). With 2FA enabled, you need both your password and a code from your authenticator app to log in. It is like having two locks on your front door.
:::

:::callout{type=warning}
We strongly recommend enabling 2FA. It protects your account even if someone discovers your password.
:::

:::step{number=3 icon=Palette title="Branding" navigation="Sidebar > Account > Branding Settings"}
Make your portal look like your own brand! Upload your logo, set your brand colors, and customize your favicon. Your clients will see your branding when they log in -- they will never see the underlying platform branding.
:::

:::screenshot{caption="Your Branding customization page"}
:::

:::callout{type=tip}
Choose colors that match your company's brand guidelines. The primary color is used for buttons and highlights, while the secondary color is used for accents.
:::

:::step{number=4 icon=Users title="Team Management" navigation="Sidebar > Customer Management > Tenant Users"}
Invite colleagues to help manage your cloud business. Each team member gets their own login with a role that controls what they can access (owner, admin, member, or billing).
:::
