---
title: Managing Clients & Leads
subtitle: Your clients are the businesses and people who use your cloud services. Leads are potential clients you are still talking to. Think of clients as your customers and leads as people browsing your shop.

:::step{number=1 icon=Users title="View Your Clients" navigation="Sidebar > Customer Management > Clients"}
The clients page shows everyone who has an account with you. You can see their name, email, status, and how many resources they are using. It is your customer directory.
:::

:::screenshot{caption="Your Clients list page"}
:::

:::step{number=2 icon=UserPlus title="Add a New Client" navigation="Sidebar > Customer Management > Clients > New"}
Click "New" to add a client manually. Fill in their company name, contact email, and any initial settings. They will receive an invitation email to set up their account and start using your services.
:::

:::screenshot{caption="Your Create Client form"}
:::

:::callout{type=tip}
Clients can also sign up on their own through your branded sign-up page. You do not always need to create them manually.
:::

:::step{number=3 icon=Target title="Sales Leads" navigation="Sidebar > Customer Management > Leads"}
Leads are potential customers in your sales pipeline. Track them from first contact through to becoming a paying client. Each lead has a stage (new, qualified, proposal, negotiation, won, lost).
:::

:::step{number=4 icon=Handshake title="Partners" navigation="Sidebar > Customer Management > Partners"}
Partners are other businesses you work with -- they might refer clients to you or resell your services. Add and manage your partner relationships here.
:::

:::step{number=5 icon=UserCog title="Team Members" navigation="Sidebar > Customer Management > Tenant Users"}
Invite people from your organization to help manage your cloud business. Each team member gets their own login with permissions you define. Think of it like giving your employees access badges to different parts of the office.
:::
