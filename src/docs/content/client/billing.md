:::step{number=3 icon=CreditCard title="Making a Payment" navigation="Click Pay on any pending invoice"}
When you have an outstanding balance, click "Pay" to complete the payment. You will be guided through a secure payment process. Your payment information is always encrypted and safe.
:::

:::step{number=4 icon=FileText title="Billing Overview" navigation="Sidebar > Billing"}
The billing page shows your current balance, payment methods, and billing preferences. Set up your preferred payment method here so future charges are handled smoothly.
:::

:::callout{type=info}
You are billed based on what you use. Running servers, stored data, and network traffic all contribute to your bill. Stop servers you are not using to save money!
:::

## Upgrading & Resize Costs

:::step{number=5 icon=ArrowUpDown title="How Resize Billing Works"}
When you upgrade a server to a bigger size (more power, more storage), you do not pay the full new price immediately. Instead, you only pay the **difference** between what you are currently paying and the new price, adjusted for how much time is left in your billing period.

Think of it like upgrading your phone plan mid-month -- you only pay the extra cost for the remaining days, not the whole month.
:::

:::callout{type=tip}
Use the **Pricing Calculator** before resizing to see exactly what the prorated cost will be. No surprises!
:::
