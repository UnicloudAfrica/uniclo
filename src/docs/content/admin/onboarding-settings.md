:::step{number=1 icon=Settings title="Open Onboarding Settings" navigation="Sidebar > Onboarding > Onboarding Settings"}
The onboarding settings page lets you customize what new tenants see and do during setup. You control which steps are required, what information they need to provide, and what verification is needed before they can go live.
:::

:::screenshot{caption="Your Onboarding Settings page"}
:::

:::step{number=2 icon=ListChecks title="Configure Steps" navigation="Sidebar > Onboarding > Onboarding Settings"}
Define the onboarding steps — for example, company profile, branding (logo and colors), billing setup, and initial region selection. You can reorder steps, make them required or optional, and add custom fields for any information you need from new tenants.
:::

:::step{number=3 icon=Eye title="Preview the Onboarding Flow" navigation="Sidebar > Onboarding > Onboarding Settings > Preview"}
Preview what tenants will see during onboarding. This helps you ensure the flow is clear and intuitive before any real tenant goes through it.
:::

:::callout{type=tip}
Keep the onboarding process short and focused. The faster tenants can complete setup, the sooner they start using the platform and generating revenue.
:::
