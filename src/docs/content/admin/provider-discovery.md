:::step{number=1 icon=Search title="Discover Providers" navigation="Sidebar > Provider Discovery"}
The provider discovery page lets you connect to and explore available cloud infrastructure. Once connected, you can see what resources, regions, and capabilities are available. Think of it as scouting new locations to expand your platform.
:::

:::screenshot{caption="Your Provider Discovery page"}
:::

:::step{number=2 icon=RefreshCw title="Sync Users & Projects" navigation="Sidebar > Provider Discovery > Sync"}
After connecting to a provider, you can sync existing users and projects into your platform. This pulls in the current state of resources so everything is tracked in one place. Syncing keeps your platform's view consistent with what actually exists in the infrastructure.
:::

:::step{number=3 icon=Settings title="Configure Integration" navigation="Sidebar > Provider Discovery > Settings"}
Set up how the platform communicates with the connected infrastructure — connection credentials, sync frequency, and which resources to import. Proper configuration ensures a reliable and up-to-date integration.
:::

:::callout{type=info}
Provider discovery is typically a one-time setup when connecting new infrastructure. After the initial sync, the platform keeps everything updated automatically.
:::
