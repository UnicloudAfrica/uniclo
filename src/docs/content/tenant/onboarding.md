:::step{number=1 icon=Compass title="Start the Onboarding Wizard" navigation="Sidebar > Regional > Onboarding"}
When you first access your account, the onboarding wizard guides you through the key setup steps. It appears automatically, but you can also return to it at any time from the sidebar.
:::

:::screenshot{caption="Your Onboarding wizard welcome screen"}
:::

:::step{number=2 icon=Building title="Set Up Your Organization"}
The first step is entering your organization details -- company name, contact information, and business type. This information appears on invoices and in your branded client portal.
:::

:::step{number=3 icon=MapPin title="Select Your Regions"}
Choose which data center regions you want to operate in. You can always add more regions later as your business grows. Picking regions close to your clients gives them the best performance.
:::

:::screenshot{caption="Your Region selection step"}
:::

:::callout{type=info}
You do not have to complete every onboarding step in one sitting. Your progress is saved automatically, and you can pick up where you left off at any time.
:::

:::step{number=4 icon=CheckCircle title="Review and Finish"}
At the end of the wizard, review your choices and confirm. Once onboarding is complete, your dashboard unlocks all features and you are ready to start adding clients and deploying resources.
:::
