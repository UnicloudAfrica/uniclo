:::step{number=1 icon=Settings title="Profile Settings" navigation="Sidebar > Account Settings"}
Update your personal information -- name, email, phone number, and company details. Make sure your email is correct, as that is how you receive important notifications and password resets.
:::

:::screenshot{caption="Your Account Settings page"}
:::

:::step{number=2 icon=Shield title="Password & Two-Factor Authentication" navigation="Account Settings > Security"}
Keep your account secure. Change your password regularly and enable two-factor authentication (2FA) for an extra layer of protection. With 2FA, even if someone guesses your password, they still cannot get in without the code from your authenticator app.
:::

:::callout{type=warning}
Always enable 2FA! It is the single most important thing you can do to protect your account. It takes 30 seconds to set up and could save you from a major security incident.
:::

:::step{number=3 icon=Users title="Team Management" navigation="Sidebar > Team"}
Invite colleagues to your account so they can help manage your cloud resources. Each team member gets their own login with specific permissions:

- **Owner** -- Full access to everything (that is you!)
- **Admin** -- Can manage most things except billing
- **Member** -- Can view and manage servers
- **Billing** -- Can only see and manage billing
  :::

:::screenshot{caption="Your Team management page"}
:::

:::callout{type=tip}
Give each team member only the access they need. A developer probably does not need billing access, and an accountant probably does not need to manage servers.
:::
